Vision and Mission

Vision

National Institute of Global Education and Technology (NIGET), a premier educational institution, is an academic fraternity of individuals dedicated to the motto of 'EXCELLENCE AND SERVICE.'

We strive to reach out to the star of perfection through an earnest academic pursuit for 'excellence,' and our efforts blossom into 'service' through our creative and empathetic involvement in the society to transform it.

Education prepares one to face the challenges of life by bringing out the best in him/her. If this is well accepted, education should be relevant to the needs of the time and address the problems of the day. Being inspired by Blessed Kuriakose Elias Chavara, the founder of Carmelites of Mary Immaculate and the pioneer in innovative education, National Institute of Global Education and Technology (NIGET) was proactive to define and redefine its mission and strategies reading the signs of the time.

Mission

"National Institute of Global Education and Technology (NIGET) is a nurturing ground for an individual's futuristic development to make an effective contribution to the society in a dynamic environment."
